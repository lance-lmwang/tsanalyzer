# Product Guidelines

## Prose Style
- **Formal and Precise**: Use professional, objective language. Avoid colloquialisms or marketing fluff.
- **Instrument-Grade Terminology**: Use standard-compliant terms (MPEG-TS, P1/P2/P3, V-STC).
- **Conciseness**: Prioritize clarity and technical accuracy over length.

## UX Principles
- **Clarity Above All**: Ensure all measurement results are unambiguous and easily verifiable.
- **Verification First**: Every analysis result must include its source and method of derivation.
- **Speed of Insight**: Design for rapid fault isolation; the user must understand the problem within seconds.
- **Forensic Accuracy**: Maintain the integrity of the data; avoid any non-deterministic approximations.

## Design Guidelines
- **Instrument Aesthetics**: Use clear, high-contrast layouts for visual data representation.
- **Standard Alignment**: Adhere to established broadcast and telecommunications industry UI/UX standards.
- **Deterministic Reporting**: All reports must include a hash of the engine version and the source capture for complete traceability.
