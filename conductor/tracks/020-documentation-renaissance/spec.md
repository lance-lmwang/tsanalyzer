# Specification: Documentation Renaissance

## 1. Objective
Transform TsAnalyzer's documentation into a world-class, comprehensive technical library. The goal is to ensure TsAnalyzer meets the strict standards of Vimeo (DX), Cloudinary (Scale), Mum (Compliance), and Bitmovin (Interoperability & Authority).

## 2. Requirements (World-Class Standards)
- **100% Discoverability**: Logical pillar-based indexing in `README.md`.
- **Developer Experience**: Guide-style Lua manuals and copy-pasteable examples.
- **Operational Excellence**: Actionable Runbooks and K8s best practices.
- **Corporate Trust**: SBOM, formal Security Policy, and SemVer versioning.
- **Ecosystem Authority**: Manifest-to-Packet metadata correlation and hardware-level benchmarking baselines.

## 3. Scope
- **Foundation**: `README.md` Re-org, `docs/glossary.md`.
- **Programmability**: `docs/05_pipeline/lua_scripting_guide.md`.
- **Cloud & Infra**: `docs/03_deployment/`, `kubernetes_best_practices.md`, `hardware_compatibility.md`.
- **Operations & Scale**: `docs/04_operations/troubleshooting_runbook.md`, `cloud_archive_logic.md`.
- **Compliance & Governance**: `SECURITY.md`, `docs/08_compliance/SBOM.md`, `versioning_policy.md`.
- **Ecosystem Alignment (Bitmovin Std)**: `docs/02_metrology/manifest_metadata_correlation.md`, `docs/07_validation/standard_comparison_baseline.md`.

## 4. Quality Standards
- No broken links.
- LaTeX formulas for all metrology math.
- Actionable advice for every error state.
- Verified hardware performance benchmarks.
