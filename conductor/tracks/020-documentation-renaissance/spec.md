# Specification: Documentation Renaissance

## 1. Objective
Transform TsAnalyzer's documentation into a world-class, comprehensive technical library. The goal is to ensure TsAnalyzer meets the strict Developer Experience (DX) of Vimeo, the Cloud-Native Scalability of Cloudinary, and the Corporate Compliance (Legal/Security) standards of Mum Group.

## 2. Requirements (Global Industry Standards)
- **100% Discoverability**: Logical pillar-based indexing in `README.md`.
- **Vimeo Std (DX & Ops)**: 5-minute onboarding and an actionable Operational Runbook for NOC teams.
- **Cloudinary Std (Scale)**: K8s best practices, S3 forensic automation, and structured JSON log schemas.
- **Mum Std (Trust & Compliance)**: Software Bill of Materials (SBOM), formal SECURITY policy, and SemVer-based Change Management.

## 3. Scope
- **Foundation**: `README.md` Re-org, `docs/glossary.md`.
- **Gateway Bible**: `docs/05_pipeline/lua_scripting_guide.md`.
- **Infrastructure**: `docs/03_deployment/deployment_guide.md`, `kubernetes_best_practices.md`, `hardware_compatibility.md`.
- **Operations**: `docs/04_operations/troubleshooting_runbook.md`, `cloud_archive_logic.md`, `best_practice_blueprints.md`.
- **Compliance & Governance**: `SECURITY.md`, `docs/08_compliance/SBOM.md`, `versioning_policy.md`, `CHANGELOG.md`.

## 4. Quality Standards
- No broken links.
- Actionable mitigation for all error states.
- 100% Transparency on third-party licenses.
- Consistent LaTeX formatting for metrology math.
